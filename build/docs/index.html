What is Rewards Credit Card API?

Rewards Credit Card API is the most complete and current API for searching and discovering detailed information about credit cards.

One dataset to rule them all

The most detailed and comprehensive collection of credit card metadata commercially available. We cover 99.5% of the credit card market in the United States.

Over 25 API Endpoints

Chances are there is an endpoint that will meet your need without requring much change to your application. More endpoints in development.

Complete list of credit card spend categories

Want to know which cards offer 3% on dining? Are there any limits or caveats? We have the most complete dataset of credit card spend bonus category information.

Custom endpoints

Can't find an endpoint that meets your needs? Let us know and we'll work on a solution that does! Want a card or two added? No problem, we'll get it in there within a day.

Tech Support

With so much data available it can quite overwhelming, send us an email and we'll get right back to you with whatever question you have.

Find the best sign-up bonuses

Our sign-up bonus endpoints give you the ability to search through bank's offerings for airline miles, hotel point, and cash bonuses.

Simple REST API

Our API is organized around REST, and allows for very simple integrations with your application. We are constantly improving the API to add more features and data points.

Card image

We have images for over 95% of the cards. We also host the images on our servers so there's no need to be concerned about bad links.

Bank and Credit Union Data

In addition to credit card data we offer bank data pulled direct from the FDIC and credit union data sourced from the NCUA.

Individual card details

Our card detail endpoint provides everything you want to know about a card - from obscure credits to sign-up bonus to annual spend bonuses to individual card url's.

Partnership with Microsoft Azure

We run on Microsoft Azure and have been working with Microsoft for over 20 years. Because of this we have four-nines availability - 99.99% uptime

Updates daily

The credit card space is constantly evolving and changing - our processes catch these updates daily and refresh your data.
